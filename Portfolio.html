Our Building Process

At 44 Studios, we follow a structured process to ensure your website meets your needs and exceeds your expectations:

  • Consultation: We start with a detailed discussion to understand your goals, target audience, and design preferences.
  • Planning: We create a project plan outlining the site structure, features, and timeline.
  • Design: Our designers craft a visually appealing layout that aligns with your brand identity.
  • Development: Our developers bring the design to life using the latest technologies and best practices.
  • Testing: We rigorously test the website for functionality, performance, and responsiveness across devices.
  • Launch: Once approved, we launch your website and ensure everything runs smoothly.
  • Support: We offer ongoing support and maintenance to keep your site updated and secure.
